# Title: Dog Poop Casserole
## Ingredients:
  - 1 tsp Salt
  - 1 tsp Pepper
  - 1 steaming pile of dog poop
## Directions:
  - Combine ingredients  
  - Bake at 375° F (190° C) for 11 minutes
  - Smell for doneness
## Yield:
  - Serves infinitely many people because
  - portion size is zero!

## Enjoy!
